# ToolTok Play Store Listing

## App Name

ToolTok

## Short Description

Curated AI tool videos, workflow ideas, and useful creator picks.

## Full Description

ToolTok is a curated short-form discovery app for people who care about useful workflows, not empty hype.

Open the live ToolTok experience in a fast Android shell and browse practical videos covering AI tools, creator systems, automation, and real-world workflows. Every feed item is selected to help users find higher-signal ideas faster.

What users can do in ToolTok:

- Browse a curated video feed focused on useful tools and workflows
- Open matching tool recommendations with clearly labeled outbound links
- Read summaries and follow related workflow ideas
- Subscribe to the weekly digest
- Open the same live ToolTok experience available on the web

ToolTok is designed to stay lightweight while still giving users a mobile-first way to explore the live product. The Android app connects directly to the hosted ToolTok service at `https://tooltok.vercel.app/`.
